How EcoSwap Works

1. List Your Items

Take photos and describe items you no longer need. It's free and takes just minutes.

2. Discover & Connect

Browse items in your area, message sellers, and negotiate fair swaps or purchases.

3. Meet & Exchange

Meet safely in public places, complete your swap, and help build a sustainable community.

4. Save the Planet

Track your environmental impact and see how much CO2 you've saved through sustainable swapping.
